Taylor's Version Announcements

Taylor surprise anounced the release of her re-recorded album, "1989 (Taylor's Version)" in August 2023. She did the same for "Speak Now (Taylor's Version)" earlier that summer in May.

Music Video Premiere

On the release day of Speak Now TV, Taylor surprise premiered the music video for her vault song "I Can See You" at her Kansas City concert. She brought the cast of said video on stage afterwards, which included actors Joey King and Taylor Lautner.

Ice Spice Appearance

Taylor brought out Ice Spice to perform their remix of Swift's song "Karma" together after live-premiering the music video.

Rain Shows

Taylor is the definition of "the show must go on." To know Taylor is to know how much she loves a rain show, and she got her fair share of those during the span of this tour. One show in particular was raining so much that some of her equipment got ruined in the process.

Bianka Bryant Gets the 22 Hat

The daughter of late NBA legend Kobe Bryant was the recipient of the 22 hat in Los Angeles. This moment was featured in the concert film.

The Eras Tour Movie

Taylor released a concert film for the tour on October 13 (there's that number again!) of 2023. The film was released in theaters and fans could purchase a special Eras-themed souvenier popcorn bucket and cup.

The Tortured Poets Department Set Added

After Swift released her 11th studio album back in April, she debuted her shiny new set for this new era at the next show. Fans speculated whether she would make this move or not, and while exciting it did come at the expense of a few songs in other eras.

Travis Kelce Stage Debut

The timeline of Taylor and Travis' relationship has been a wild ride, but no one expected to see the Chief's star on the Eras Tour stage. He dressed up, got into character, and even carried an "unconcious" Swift off the stage.
